Thank you for participating in this experiment. Please read the following instructions carefully.

There are no right or wrong answers in this experiment - we are only interested in your personal opinion.

This experiment primarily consists of a mental arithmetic (2 rounds) and two typing tasks (3 rounds). You will complete the typing task alone once, and in a team of three people once. Each task round is followed by a short questionnaire.

The experiment will take approximately 60 minutes.

If you have any questions about the experiment please contact Dr. Michael Knierim (michael.knierim@kit.edu) at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).
